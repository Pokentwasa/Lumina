PENTHOUSE — V&A WATERFRONT, CAPE TOWN — 2023

OasisPenthouse

ClientPrivate Residential
LocationV&A Waterfront, Cape Town
Year2023
ScopeInterior Design, Landscape, Lighting
Area480 m²

Stillness abovethe harbour.

The Oasis Penthouse occupies the full top floor of a V&A Waterfront tower, with 270-degree views of Table Mountain, the harbour, and the Atlantic. The client's brief was a single word: calm. After years of international travel and corporate intensity, they wanted a home that felt like decompression the moment they walked through the door.

We stripped the existing fit-out entirely. Gone were the marble features, the recessed ceilings, the generic luxury finishes. In their place: raw plaster, aged oak, and a palette of warm greys that shifts tone with the Cape Town light throughout the day.

THE ROOFTOP

A gardenabove the city.

The 180m² rooftop terrace was transformed into a planted landscape — olive trees, restio grasses, and drought-resistant succulents in raised planters of weathered steel. A 12-metre lap pool runs the length of the northern edge. The pool water is treated without chlorine, using a natural mineral system that leaves it silky rather than clinical.
